Existing Conditions Report — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. Having completed your research and presented preliminary findings, you will now write the first formal section of your plan: the existing conditions analysis.

Assignment: Write up the first section of the report, which includes your assessment and existing conditions analysis. This written report should synthesize your research findings and provide the factual and analytical foundation for the plan recommendations that follow. Your report should address:

1. Environmental conditions: What are the primary pollution sources, environmental risks, and contamination issues in Fields Corner? What is the environmental impact of the auto-related business cluster and other land uses?

2. Economic conditions: What is the economic base of the neighborhood? What industries and clusters exist, and what are their inter-relationships? What are the employment levels, income levels, and wealth-building opportunities for residents?

3. Market conditions: What markets are currently served by local businesses? What unmet needs or expansion opportunities exist?

4. Environmental justice: What are the key environmental justice concerns for Fields Corner residents, particularly the Vietnamese immigrant community?

5. Implications: What do these existing conditions mean for Viet-AID's project goals? Which conditions present the greatest challenges, and which present the best opportunities?
