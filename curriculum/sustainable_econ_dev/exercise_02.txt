Waste Streams/Emissions and Industry Cluster Analysis — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. Fields Corner has significant environmental challenges — particularly from its concentration of auto-related businesses — and a distinct economic cluster of Vietnamese-owned businesses. Viet-AID aims to address environmental and economic conditions simultaneously, working with existing businesses while creating new opportunities.

Assignment: Each team (or a designated sub-group) should discuss and prepare a brief (2 to 3-page) outline or summary of how you plan to apply waste stream, emissions, and industry cluster analysis to the Fields Corner project. Address the following questions:

1. What appear to be the primary waste/emission sources for Fields Corner?

2. Which ones are most significant given the neighborhood and project goals? Which present the best opportunities to address within the project?

3. What are the primary industries and economic clusters in the neighborhood and project area? What appear to be their primary inter-relationships or common factor needs?

4. Which industries or clusters are significant sources of emissions or other environmental risks?

5. Which ones are most important to the neighborhood and Viet-AID's project goals?

6. What additional information is needed to evaluate and propose an agenda around waste stream and industry/cluster issues?

7. What is the plan to collect and analyze this information?
