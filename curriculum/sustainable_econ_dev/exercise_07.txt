Brownfields and Real Estate Development — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. Fields Corner has significant contamination and environmental blight from auto-related land uses, and Viet-AID's goals include addressing these conditions to unlock economic opportunity for the neighborhood.

Assignment: Each team (or a designated sub-group) should discuss and write a brief (2 to 3-page) outline or summary of your initial thoughts on how to apply brownfields cleanup and real estate development policies and practices to the Fields Corner project. Address the following questions:

1. What opportunities exist to apply brownfields remediation or real estate development approaches within the project, and what are the desired outcomes that will result?

2. What are the critical barriers faced in utilizing these approaches in Fields Corner?

3. What are your initial ideas about how to apply them to the project?

4. What capacities, resources, and relationships are needed to implement your ideas?

5. What additional information, research, or analysis is needed to develop and test these components of the plan?
