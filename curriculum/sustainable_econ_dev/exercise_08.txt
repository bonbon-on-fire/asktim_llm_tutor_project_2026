Small Business Development and Land Use Planning — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. Viet-AID's core mission includes promoting economic self-sufficiency for Vietnamese immigrants through business development, and Fields Corner's Vietnamese business district is a central asset in that effort.

Assignment: Each team (or a designated sub-group) should discuss and write a brief (2 to 3-page) outline or summary of your initial thoughts on how to apply small business development and land use planning policies and practices to the Fields Corner project. Address the following questions:

1. What opportunities exist to apply small business development or land use planning approaches within the project, and what are the desired outcomes that will result?

2. What are Viet-AID's and other organizations' existing activities in these areas, and are there important gaps or areas for improvement or extension of this work?

3. What are your initial ideas about how to apply or extend these approaches to advance Viet-AID's and the community's goals?

4. What capacities, resources, and relationships are needed to implement these ideas?

5. What additional information, research, or analysis is needed to develop and test these components of the plan?

Note: Students should meet with Viet-AID this week to review their proposals and plan elements and gain feedback on them.
