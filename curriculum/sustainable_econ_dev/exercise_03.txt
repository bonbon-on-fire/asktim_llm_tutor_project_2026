Environmental Justice and Market Analysis — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. Fields Corner is one of the most diverse and lowest-income neighborhoods in Boston, with a history of disinvestment, environmental pollution from auto-related uses, and an active Vietnamese business district. Viet-AID's goals include expanding employment, improving environmental conditions, and building economic capacity for Vietnamese entrepreneurs.

Assignment: Each team (or a designated sub-group) should discuss and prepare a brief (2 to 3-page) outline or summary of how you plan to apply environmental justice analysis and market analysis to the Fields Corner project. Address the following questions:

1. What are the important environmental justice issues for Fields Corner?

2. What markets are now served by local businesses, and what are good market expansion opportunities?

3. Which environmental justice issues and market opportunities are most significant given the neighborhood and project goals? Which present the best opportunities to address within the project?

4. What additional information is needed to evaluate and propose an agenda around these issues?

5. What is the plan to collect and analyze this information?
