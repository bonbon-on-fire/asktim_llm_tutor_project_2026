Technology Transfer, Green Design, and Industrial Ecology — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. You have now completed your existing conditions analysis. This phase focuses on identifying specific policy tools and interventions — specifically technology transfer, green design, and industrial ecology approaches — that could advance Viet-AID's goals.

Assignment: Each team (or a designated sub-group) should discuss and write a brief (2 to 3-page) outline or summary of your initial thoughts on how to apply technology-based policies and practices to the Fields Corner project. Address the following questions:

1. What opportunities exist to apply technology transfer, green design, or industrial ecology approaches within the project, and what are the desired outcomes that would result?

2. What are the critical barriers faced in utilizing these approaches in Fields Corner or with Viet-AID's target businesses?

3. What are your initial ideas about how to apply them to the project?

4. What capacities, resources, and relationships are needed to apply your ideas for these technologies and technology transfer tools?

5. What additional information, research, or analysis is needed to develop and test these components of the plan?
