Preliminary Findings Presentation — Viet-AID / Fields Corner

Your team is working with Viet-AID to develop a sustainable development plan for the Fields Corner neighborhood of Dorchester, Boston. You have now completed your existing conditions research and are ready to share your findings with the class and your client.

Assignment: Each project team will have 30 to 40 minutes to present their existing conditions findings, the implications for the project, and initial proposals for the plan focus and agenda. Your presentation should address:

1. What are the key environmental conditions in Fields Corner — pollution sources, contamination, auto-related impacts — and what do they mean for the project?

2. What are the key economic conditions — employment, business clusters, market opportunities, disinvestment patterns — and how do they relate to Viet-AID's goals?

3. What environmental justice issues are most significant for the neighborhood and Vietnamese community?

4. Based on your findings, what is your initial proposal for the focus and agenda of the sustainable development plan?

5. What are the most important open questions or areas where you need additional information or client feedback?

Note: Class representatives should meet with Viet-AID during this week to discuss and get feedback on initial findings and the proposed plan agenda.
