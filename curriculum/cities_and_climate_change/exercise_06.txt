City Case Study Investigation — Climate Action Summary

Recommended due date: July 18 by 11:59 EST. Final due date: August 29 by 11:59 PM EST.

This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by the course instructors for content, but no direct instructor feedback will be offered this week. A discussion forum will be offered to allow you to exchange and discuss findings with your fellow learners as a peer learning opportunity.

Assignment Overview
This week you will research and summarize your city's current climate action efforts using available reports and documentation.

Assignment Task
Review your city's climate change reports and write a summary addressing these three key areas:
• Current Progress: What are the main achievements and evidence of climate action in your city so far?
• Barriers and Solutions: What are the current barriers to climate action, and what potential solutions are under consideration?
• Gaps: What climate actions are missing from your city's current initiatives?

Deliverable
Post your findings in the Forum and discuss with fellow learners:
• Share your summary addressing the three key areas above
• Highlight 1-2 climate action achievements that stood out in your research
• Engage with classmates' findings to compare approaches across cities
