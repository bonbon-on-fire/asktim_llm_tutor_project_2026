City Case Study Investigation — Spider Diagram

Recommended due date: August 1 by 11:59 EST. Final due date: August 29 by 11:59 PM EST.

This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by the course instructors for content. For Certificate learners, the instructors will select a sample of submissions to summarize with feedback in an uploaded video. A discussion forum will be offered to all learners to allow you to exchange and discuss findings with your fellow learners as a peer learning opportunity.

Assignment Overview
This week you will complete a Spider Diagram to map out climate action in your case study city as it relates to the 5 Pathways for Urban Transformation.

Assignment Options
The Spider Diagram activity can be completed in two different ways, which will be described in detail on the following pages:

Option 1: Use the Spider Diagram table (see example on next pages) to write out the information you find about your city (preferred).
• To complete this option, you will copy and paste a table template into your submission

Option 2: Use the Spider Diagram diagram (see example on next pages) to draw out the information you find about your city.
• To complete this option, you will download, print and complete the map by hand, then scan it in to add it to your submission

After you complete the submission in one of the two ways above, you will also be required to participate in the discussion forum with your fellow learners. The prompt will be found on the pages that follow.
