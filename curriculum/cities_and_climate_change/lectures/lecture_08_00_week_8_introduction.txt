PROFESSOR: Welcome to week eight, case studies.
This week, we will explore a series of case studies
on global cities that are addressing climate challenges
using mitigation techniques and adaptation
strategies to climate change.
Case study cities include Kano, Nigeria; Copenhagen, Denmark;
and Mombasa, Kenya.
Your assignment for Week 8 will be
to complete a spider diagram that
maps out climate action in your case study city
using the six pathways of urban transformation
and evaluates the process of climate action
and implementation.
