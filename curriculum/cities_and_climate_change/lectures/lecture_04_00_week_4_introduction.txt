
PROFESSOR: Welcome to Cities and Climate Change,
week four on Housing Informality and Social Dimensions
of Climate Change.
This week, we will start with an introduction
to the social dimensions of climate change with Janelle
Knox Hayes.
We will then explore challenges of housing in relation
to a changing climate, hearing from experts
such as Zi Chi Zhang, Deborah Bock, and Radley Horton.
Juan Camilo Osorio will then discuss
how cities can develop just transitions
in the face of climate change.
In your assignment for week four,
you will develop a power actors map for your case study city,
exploring who the key actors are involved
in climate action, what their role of involvement is,
and how they are connected to one another.
