
PROFESSOR: Welcome to Cities and Climate Change Week
11 on drafting a climate action plan.
During Week 11, we will explore processes
for drafting a climate action plan, including
how your case study research informs this process.
You will learn from leading experts on case study cities,
including Emma Porrio from Manila in the Philippines
and Barbara Norman from Canberra, Australia.
Your assignment for this week will
be to reflect on your previous SWOT analysis
to begin developing a climate action plan prospectus.
Friendly reminder that the SWOT analysis
is composed of four key components on strengths,
weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
