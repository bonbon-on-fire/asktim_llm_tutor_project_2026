PROFESSOR: Welcome to week three, Urban Energy
and Transportation.
This week, we will introduce you to urban energy systems,
exploring transitioning to decarbonized energy
and alternative energy systems.
We will hear from Chuck Kutcher and Anne White.
We will also hear from Larry Susskind and Janelle Knox-Hayes
on energy initiatives and the need
for community engagement and buy-in
to support energy transitions.
Jim Aloisi will introduce us to transportation systems,
discussing sustainability and transitioning
to a decarbonized transit network.
Minal Pathak will share her expertise
on transportation systems and alternatives
in Ahmedabad, India.
Your assignment for week three will be to explore and collect
data on the leading climate actors, as well as those yet
to be implemented in your case study city.
