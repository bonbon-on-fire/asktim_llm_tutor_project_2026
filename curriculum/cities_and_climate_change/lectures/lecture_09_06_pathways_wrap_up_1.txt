PROFESSOR: Transformations involve
large, abrupt, and persistent changes
in the structure and function of physical and/or social systems.
What we've learned throughout this course
is that cities may not be able to address challenges
and fulfill climate change leadership potential
without transformation.
Urban transformation integrates mitigation and adaptation
in virtually every decision, plan,
and implementation in cities.
The six urban transformation pathways
that were covered throughout the course,
provide a foundational framework for the successful development
and implementation of climate action in cities.
Cities that are making progress in transformative climate
change actions are following many or all of these pathways.
And cities who are preparing now for progress
can utilize these pathways in climate change action,
planning, and implementation.
