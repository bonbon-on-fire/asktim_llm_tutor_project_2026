PROFESSOR: Welcome to week 5, Climate Action.
This week, we will be exploring the six pathways
to urban transformation, which provide
a foundational framework for the successful development
and implementation of climate action in cities.
We will also hear from Jeffrey Raven and Mattia Leone
on urban design and planning and their relationship
to climate change and adaptation and mitigation.
Gina Ziervogel will introduce us to equitable adaptation,
and Diana Reckien will share her expertise
on the quality of urban adaptation planning.
Your assignment for the week will
be to complete a peer assessment of one of your fellow learner's
power actor maps from week 4.
