City Case Study Investigation — Draft Climate Action Plan Prospectus

Recommended due date: August 22 by 11:59 EST. Final due date: August 29 by 11:59 PM EST.

This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by the course instructors for content, but will not include direct instructor feedback. A discussion forum will be offered to all learners to allow you to exchange and discuss findings with your fellow learners for a peer learning opportunity.

Assignment Overview
Create a DRAFT of 2 Climate Action Plan components for your case study city, building on your SWOT Analysis and findings about barriers to climate action. Each component should be 200-300 words. You'll share your research in the forum discussion and submit the final prospectus in Week 12.

When developing your policy recommendations, focus on Environmental Justice implications, vulnerabilities and environmental stressors in your city, and overlaps between different Climate Action Plan components.

Component Structure
For each of your 2 components, include the following sections:
COMPONENT [1 or 2]: [Policy Name]
• Context - Current situation and key challenge
• Opportunity - Specific opportunity for action
• Policy Recommendation - 200-300 words (your complete component)
• EJ Implications - Impact on vulnerable communities and equity measures
• Interactions - How this supports other climate actions and potential synergies

Review the examples of a Draft Climate Action Plan Prospectus below for reference and before completing this worksheet. Use your completed components for forum discussion to refine your approach before the final submission.
