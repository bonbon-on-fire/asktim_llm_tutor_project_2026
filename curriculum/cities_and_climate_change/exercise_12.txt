City Case Study Investigation — Final Climate Action Plan Prospectus

Assignment due August 29 by 11:59 PM EST.

This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by course instructors for content, and will include direct instructor feedback for Certificate learners through a summary feedback video. A discussion forum will be offered to all learners to allow you to exchange and discuss findings with your fellow learners for a peer learning opportunity.

Assignment Overview
This week you will complete your Climate Action Plan Prospectus. Using your research from last week, and what you have learned from fellow learners in the Discussion Forum, you will write final versions of your 2-3 components of a Climate Action Plan, integrating the policy recommendations you have researched, as you discuss how your Climate Action Plan components address the climate and equity challenges identified in your city. Consider the actors that need to be involved in decision-making processes, and how your policy recommendations make space for their contributions.

Assignment Completion Process
You will complete this assignment in the following Graded Response, however your grade will be a self-assessment exercise. Follow the instructions in the ORA for how to complete a Self-Assessment. Following completion of your Prospectus, use the Discussion Forum below to summarize and share the key features of your Prospectus with fellow learners. Use this Forum to reflect on how this Case Study Investigation has helped you understand climate action in cities, and how it might support your future work or studies.
