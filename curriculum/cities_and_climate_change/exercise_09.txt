This assignment is required for Certificate learners, but all learners are encouraged to complete the City Case Study Investigations.

This assignment will be reviewed by the course instructors for content, but will not include direct instructor feedback. A discussion forum will be offered to all learners to allow you to exchange and discuss findings with your fellow learners for a peer learning opportunity.

Assignment Overview
You will design alternative Climate Action Plan components for your case study city, building on your research from Modules 1 and 2. This is NOT an analysis of existing plans, but your own recommendations based on your city's specific needs.

Step 1: Component Selection
Choose exactly 3 components from the Climate Action Plan Components diagram posted below:
• List of Climate Action Plan Components to choose from (sourced from UCCRN ARC 3.2).

Step 2: Research and Analysis
For each chosen component, write 3-5 bullet points that include:
• Your recommendation for this component
• How your previous case study research supports this choice
• How this addresses your city's specific climate challenges

Step 3: Forum Submission
Post your work using this format:
City: [Your Case Study City]
Components Chosen: [List 3 components]
Component 1: [Name]
- [Bullet point 1]
- [Bullet point 2]
- [Bullet point 3]
[Continue for all 3 components]
