Group Project Presentation

Your group will present proposed solutions to a specific development problem, posed in a specific country and at a specific scale. The presentation can take the form of a traditional PowerPoint report or a video/multimedia presentation.

Your presentation will be judged on three criteria:

1. Comprehensiveness: Does the presentation fully address the problem — its causes, context, and the range of relevant considerations?

2. Creativity: Does the presentation offer innovative or non-obvious solutions that go beyond conventional approaches?

3. Feasibility: Are the proposed solutions realistic given the political, institutional, economic, and social constraints of the specific country and scale?
