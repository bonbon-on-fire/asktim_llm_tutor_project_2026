Reaction Paper

Write a short reaction paper (three pages) that makes a cogent analysis of the assigned readings for today's class, in light of the questions presented by the instructor. Your paper should:

1. Briefly explain the main issues raised by the topic under discussion.

2. Explain how the readings address the questions raised.

3. Identify how the readings differ from each other (if they do), and evaluate their relative strengths and weaknesses.
