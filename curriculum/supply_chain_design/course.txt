This course explores how to design and manage the physical, information, and financial flows that connect organizations across the supply chain. Using analytical frameworks, mathematical models, and real-world applications, you will learn to make strategic decisions related to supply chain network design, supply chain finance, supplier management and procurement, production and material requirements planning, distribution, Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP), and process and organizational design. By the end of the course you will be equipped to optimize supply chain networks, apply financial analysis to supply chain decisions, and lead cross-functional initiatives that balance operational excellence with strategic business objectives. This course is part of the MITx MicroMasters® Program in Supply Chain Management.
