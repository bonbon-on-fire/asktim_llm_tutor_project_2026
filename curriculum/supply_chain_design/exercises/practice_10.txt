Practice Problem 1: Mulcreavy Artisans
Mulcreavy Artisans (MA) produce hot tamales that are sold in stadiums and at large events. Because their business is project-oriented and episodic, they do not have the luxury of steady cash flow. This presents a number of problems for the firm – they often do not have the necessary cash to build up the inventory necessary to support large, single day events. Additionally, their collections are difficult – ever changing buyers, unclear and thoroughly manual payment processes, and accounts payable personnel that are hard to pin down. In some cases, their customers offer to pay "in-kind" with event tickets and merchandise. MA usually declines and eventually gets paid. Because many of the customers operate a cash business, they are usually willing to pay early provided they get financial incentives. This seems to be the only reliable aspect about their customers.
Part 1
0 points possible (ungraded)
To provide working capital to the firm, MA considered many options to finance their supply chain and chose two that could provide immediate working capital relief.
Which two should they choose?
Select two correct answers
Arranging an invoice auction
Factoring their receivables
Financing their inventory
Setting up automated payment process methods
Offering a dynamic (invoice) discount
Part 2
0 points possible (ungraded)
MA has decided to offer a dynamic discount. They know that they have best success if they can arrange payment within 2 days of the event conclusion, before all the event finances are settled. Which option should MA offer?
Select all correct answers
2% 10, Net 30
3% 2, Net 30
7% 1, Net 30
5% 5, Net 30
Part 3
0 points possible (ungraded)
Their next big project is this coming Monday night at the Boston Garden for a Red Hot Chili Peppers concert. MA expects to sell a lot! The concert operator, Cretan Ops has elected to go with the 7% 1, Net 30 Discount, and agreed to pay early. What does that mean?
Select all correct answers
MA will get paid 7% of the invoice price on day 1, with the balance due 30 days from the event.
Cretan Ops will pay MA on Tuesday for tamales on Monday.
Cretan Ops will enjoy a 3% discount for paying by the following Saturday.
MA will receive the invoice amount less 7% one day after invoice date of Monday.
