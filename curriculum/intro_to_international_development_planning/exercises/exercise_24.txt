TITLE: Key Issues: Security and Post-Conflict Development and Review

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:

Prompt:
What is the nature of the relationship between development interventions before, during and after conflict, and the factors that drive conflict itself? Does the rise of new global powers like China portend a transformation of the development-security agenda as conventionally practiced? Does it make sense to push for more and more convergence of security and development?
