TITLE: Key Issues: Aid and Development

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:
Write a 700–800 word reflection piece responding to the prompt below.

Prompt:
Is aid necessary for development and should one make a distinction between short-term and long-term aid? Is Easterly overly dismissive of aid? And is Sachs guilty of the opposite sin besides being unrealistic in this economic climate? What does one make of the transformation of aid in the light of evolving security agendas – like counter-insurgency – and the centrality of development aid in those efforts? Is there a moral case for aid and if so what is it? How does one judge the effectiveness of aid?
