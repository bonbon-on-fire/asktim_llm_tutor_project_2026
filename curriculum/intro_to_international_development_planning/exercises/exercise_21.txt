TITLE: Key Issues: Trade, Outsourcing, and Labor Standards

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:
Write a 700–800 word reflection piece responding to the prompt below.

Prompt:
Is openness to trade directly correlated to economic growth? What does the historical record say so far? Is the increasing shift to bilateral and regional trade arrangements compatible with the possibility of international trade, and with increasing commitment to labor rights? What do we make of the economic development strategies of emerging powers like China and India in terms of trade openness and commitment to labor standards? Who monitors labor standards?
