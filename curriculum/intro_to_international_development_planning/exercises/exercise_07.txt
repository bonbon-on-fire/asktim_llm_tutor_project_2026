TITLE: Economic Development Theories: New Institutionalism and Law

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:

What is the basis for the belief that law will lead to development? Is law a tool for development or is a development goal in itself? What is the meaning of institutions – can one unbundle them and if so, what is the role of law as an institution as opposed to other institutions, in development?
