TITLE: Development Theories Today in the Context of Global Crisis

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:
Write a 700–800 word reflection piece responding to the prompt below.

Prompt:
Does development have any core of agreed upon meaning today or has it become all-inclusive and therefore meaningless? How can one think of success in development terms today and which countries or communities would be role models? Is today's global crisis one created by too much markets or government or too little and if so what scale?
