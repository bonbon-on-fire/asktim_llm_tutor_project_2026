TITLE: Key Issues: Corruption and Reform of Public Institutions

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:
Write a 700–800 word reflection piece responding to the prompt below.

Prompt:
Is corruption-free development ever possible? How does one understand claims of Asian distinctiveness about corruption – the 'grease the wheel' thesis – against the Asian successes in rooting out corruption as in Hong Kong? What does corruption signify about the State, its legitimacy and the ensemble of techniques through which development interventions occur?
