TITLE: Key Issues: Governance of Complexity in a Globalized World

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:
Write a 700–800 word reflection piece responding to the prompt below.

Prompt:
What has been the impact of globalization on Planning Capacity of governments? In particular, are city planners more or less vulnerable to fluctuations in the global economy? How should local planners reap the benefits of globalization and protect against its adverse effects? What is the governance agenda and who is driving it? What is good governance and the stakes of introducing it in development planning? Is failure of governance a cause or consequence of globalization?
