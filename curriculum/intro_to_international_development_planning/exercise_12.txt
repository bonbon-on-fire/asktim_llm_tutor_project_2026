TITLE: Actors: Civil Society: NGOs and Social Movements

11.701 Introduction to International Development Planning
Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Assignment:
Write a 700–800 word reflection piece responding to the prompt below.

Prompt:
There is no doubt about the mission of NGOs for promoting development. How good are NGOs as development agents? In what circumstances are they more effective as agents of development? What are their limitations? What is the role of other civil society actors in development such as social movements? What is the boundary of civil society as an actor in particular development contexts?
